About Christopher HVAC

Our Story

Built on a Foundation of Trust & Expertise

Christopher HVAC was founded with a simple belief: every homeowner deserves honest, high-quality HVAC service at a fair price. With over 10 years of hands-on experience in the industry, Christopher started his own company to provide the kind of service he wished he could find as a homeowner.

As a solo owner-operator, Christopher personally handles every service call. There are no subcontractors, no bait-and-switch pricing, and no upsells you don't need. When you call Christopher HVAC, you get Christopher — a certified professional who takes pride in every job.

From quick repairs to full system replacements, every project gets the same attention to detail and commitment to quality. That's why the majority of our business comes from repeat customers and referrals.
